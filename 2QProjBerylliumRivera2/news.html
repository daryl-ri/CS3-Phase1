Pedri Impresses in Champions League debut

17-year-old youngster Pedri impressed in the Blaugrana's 5-1 thrashing of Hungarian champions Ferencvaros in Matchday one of the UEFA Champions League. The prodigy from Tegueste was signed by the Blaugrana last summer for around five million euros from Canarian club Las Palmas in Segunda Division. Pedri was subbed in at the 65th minute where he replaced fellow youngster and goalscorer Ansu Fati. After his performance, Barca coach Ronald Koeman will likely have a tough choice to make regarding his squad selection in the coming matches against Juventus and arch-rival Real Madrid.
